About Me

My Story

I was born and raised in San Diego and moved to the Bay Area in 2015 for college. I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2019 with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration and a minor in Nutritional Science.

Upon graduating, I started working in creative marketing for a mobile gaming company and came to discover my love for design. I’ve always been a very creative person but never really knew what UI/UX design was. After talking and learning from a few product designers, I decided to enroll myself in CareerFoundry’s UI Design program while working full-time with the goal of becoming a full-time UI designer.
